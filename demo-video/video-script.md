- Accent: #10B981 (Green)

- Text: #1E293B (Dark Gray)

### **Typography**:

- Headlines: Inter Bold, 32-48px
- Body: Inter Regular, 16-20px

- Smooth, professional transitions (0.3-0.5s)

- Cursor highlighting for important clicks
- Smooth mouse movements
